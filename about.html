Shaped in Wood

More Than Pots. A Way of Living.

At 11.11, we believe that the true beauty lies in its imperfections. Our collection of wooden objects speaks the language of time - slowly and thoughtfully through reverence. Each piece is born from a carefully chosen piece of wood, some over 70 years old.

Our commitment to authenticity is reflected in our process, each piece is carefully shaped, smoothed and polished, preserving the woods true tones and veins.

What began as a simple hobby in 2021 slowly grew into something far greater than we had imagined.

We started by channeling our creativity into stationery, diaries, planners, and notepads but the response wasn't what we had hoped for, and things didn't quite take off.

Rather than giving up, we chose to evolve. Driven by curiosity and a desire to explore new mediums, we shifted our focus to handcrafted concrete decor creating planters, trays, candle holders, and more.

We took our work to local flea markets, unsure of what to expect. To our surprise, people connected with it they supported us and spread the word.

After two years with concrete, we made a bold shift to wood crafting every detail ourselves and finally found our true material.
